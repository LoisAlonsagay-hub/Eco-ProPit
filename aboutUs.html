About Eco-ProPit

To promote and support sustainable practices
that reduce waste, conserve resources, and
protect the environment.

Mission

To promote and support sustainable practices that reduce
waste, conserve resources, and protect the environment. In
addition to advocating for laws and policies that support
sustainability, it aims to inform, empower, and encourage
communities and individuals to embrace sustainable activities.

Vision

In the future, Eco-ProPit sees people, businesses, and
communities embracing sustainable methods that preserve the
environment and decrease waste.

End Goals

            Reduce the amount of garbage that people and communities produce, 
            and promote the use of ethical waste management techniques 
            like recycling, composting, and correct disposal

            To encourage the use of renewable energy sources while 
            lowering reliance on fossil fuels, which will cut greenhouse 
            gas emissions and help combat climate change.

            To encourage the creation and acceptance of sustainable farming 
            methods that protect the environment and increase food security.

            To promote community involvement and cooperation in relation to 
            sustainability issues through collaborations with nearby companies, 
            institutions of higher learning, and community organizations.

            To promote laws and rules that promote sustainability and 
            safeguard the environment, such as requirements for renewable energy, 
            plastic bag bans, and pollution limits.
